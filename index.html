Meet the founder

Built on the job site

✝

I was raised in San Diego, California — but really, I grew up on the job site. My father, my grandfather, and my great-grandfather all built their lives in the trades. My dad owns his own finished carpentry business, and from the time I was a kid I watched what honest, hard work actually looks like up close. Attention to detail. Showing up. Doing it right when no one's watching.

Those weren't things I taught myself — they were instilled in me, and I carry them today not by my own strength but by God's grace. "Trust in the LORD with all your heart, and lean not on your own understanding." (Proverbs 3:5)

I've seen the sacrifice and sweat my family poured into blue-collar work — and how often hard-working tradesmen never see every dollar they've truly earned. So I made it my mission to build software that exposes the leaks, wins back your time, and makes sure you get paid for every ounce of work you put in.

It's just me — and that's on purpose. You're not passed off to a call center. You get one person who knows your business and stands behind the work personally.
